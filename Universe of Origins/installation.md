# Updating
- Download required files for [minimal](https://github.com/Nugget-Sk/universe-of-origins/releases/download/v2.3/OriginsMinimal-v2.3.zip) or [regular](https://github.com/Nugget-Sk/universe-of-origins/releases/download/v2.3/OriginsRegular-v2.3.zip)
- locate your minecraft folder
- extract the zip file into your minecraft folder
- overwrite everything that needs overwriting
- launch game and enjoy!
